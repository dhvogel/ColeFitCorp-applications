Testimonials

Katie Lutton

When I first heard about ColeFit, I was skeptical. Yes, founder and CEO Cole Jackob was pretty fit, but it was obviously just genetics. What could he teach me about working out? And what did ColeFit offer that all the other fitness programs didn't, except for exorbitant fees and a Byzantine membership tier structure? I'm now here to say that ColeFit changed my life. I used to be weak and puny, barely able to bench press my own body weight. Actually still can't do that. But with ColeFit, my protein smoothie consumption has grown exponentially, and I've finally kicked my terrible habit of sleeping in till a reasonable hour. Don't be put off by the creep in ColeFit Legal; the rest of the staff is good looking and a pleasure to work with. I also tried the ColeFit Travel package to stay in shape while in Puerto Rico. It's pricey, but with full days of hiking, surfing, snorkeling, and beach workouts, it's definitely worth it. Overall, 10/10, would recommend ColeFit to a friend (if I had any friends who weren't already doing ColeFit).

Kevin Leimkuhler

Ask any kid these days what they want to be when they grow up and you'll get the same cookie cutter answers: I want to be Batman, I want to be a firefighter, I want to be a doctor, I want to be Rich Fairbank. From the moment I emerged from the womb, I had my answer tattooed across my chest; I want to be Cole Fit. Every day I wake up it's my primary goal. I train day and night so that one day I can be blessed by the guidance and sheer determination of a Cole Fit workout. It has shaped my average daily life into that of a nomad, a glorious fit nomad. I travel from place to place in my soul, chipping away at my weaknesses and becoming Cole Fit.
